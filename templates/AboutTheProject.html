ABOUT OUR PROJECT

Core Idea Behind Our Project :

Our proposed model is a Seq2Seq Model that has been finetuned from T5-base and is capable of generating titles for your machine learning papers using the abstract. To train our model, we utilized a subset of the arXiv dataset, which has been categorized in the cs.AI category and consists of 40,000 articles. The proposed system offers several benefits, including the ability to model long-range dependencies, parallel processing, and effectiveness at capturing complex relationships.
